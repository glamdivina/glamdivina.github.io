Privacy Policy ✦

Last updated: January 2025  ·  Effective date: January 2025

At GlamDivina by Eenaar, your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. We will never sell your personal information to anyone. ✦

1. Who We Are

GlamDivina is an AI-powered fashion and beauty app developed by Eenaar. We can be reached at:

  • Email: support@glamdivina.com
  • Instagram: @glamdivina.app
  • Website: glamdivina.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create a GlamDivina account, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name (chosen by you)
  • Your password (stored securely — we never see it in plain text)

2.2 Photos and Images

When you use the Try It On feature, you may upload or take photos of outfits or jewellery. These photos are:

  • Sent to Anthropic's AI API for style analysis only
  • Not permanently stored on our servers after analysis
  • Never used to train AI models
  • Never shared with third parties beyond the analysis service

2.3 Messages

When you use the friend messaging feature, your messages (text, photos, and AI looks you share) are stored securely in Firebase (Google's cloud database) so your conversations are available when you open the app.

2.4 Usage Data

We track minimal usage data to run the app correctly:

  • Daily message count (to manage your free tier limit)
  • Whether you are a Pro subscriber
  • App theme preference
  • Last active date (for daily reset of free messages)

2.5 Payment Information

GlamDivina Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see, store, or handle your credit card or payment details. All billing is managed by Apple or Google directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide the GlamDivina app features (AI chat, outfit analysis, messaging)
  • Manage your account and subscription status
  • Send you support responses when you contact us
  • Improve the app based on general usage patterns
  • Reset your daily free message count

We do not use your information for advertising, selling to third parties, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Third Party Services We Use

GlamDivina uses these trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic Claude API — powers Divina's AI responses and outfit analysis. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Firebase by Google — stores your account, messages, and app data securely. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handles all payment processing for Pro subscriptions.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Firebase servers which are protected by Google's enterprise-grade security. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Access to your data is strictly limited to you and our development team.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Messages are retained so your conversations are available in the app
  • Try It On photos are not permanently stored after analysis
  • If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@glamdivina.com

8. Children's Privacy

GlamDivina is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@glamdivina.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Camera and Photo Library Access

GlamDivina requests access to your camera and photo library only for the Try It On feature and friend photo sharing. We only access your camera or photos when you actively choose to use these features. You can revoke this permission at any time in your phone's Settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of GlamDivina after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@glamdivina.com
  • Instagram: @glamdivina.app

We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 48 hours ✦
